About Samara Real Estate

Samara Real Estate & Contracting is a premier developer and contractor within the Samara Group, specializing in residential, commercial, and mixed-use projects across the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. With over 25 years of industry expertise, we deliver exceptional spaces that enhance communities and provide lasting value to investors and occupants alike.

Our comprehensive approach covers every phase of the real estate lifecycle—from initial land acquisition and feasibility studies to design, construction, and property management. We leverage deep market knowledge and strategic partnerships to navigate complex developments efficiently, ensuring on-time delivery and superior quality standards.
