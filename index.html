Corporate Overview

We Are a Trusted Family Business Driving Saudi Arabia's Growth

Samara Group is a family-based business with a portfolio of companies in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia & GCC, in addition to international investments & affiliations. Since 1992, we have directed capital, talent, and expertise toward diverse sectors across the region.

With more than 30 years in business, Samara has become a trusted business partner across Saudi Arabia & GCC. Our business strategy builds on competences and expertise gained in diverse sectors such as Commercial & Industrial Refrigeration, Real Estate Management, Healthcare Management, Collection & Receivable Management, Parking Solutions, General Construction, and Chemical Additives Products.

History & Legacy

Three Decades of
Business Excellence

1992

The Foundation

Founded by the Al-Samar family as a trading and contracting business in Riyadh. Beginning with commercial refrigeration installations and small construction projects, the firm's reputation for quality and reliability was established from its very first contracts.

2001

Healthcare & Services Expansion

Entry into healthcare management with the establishment of Saryryah Healthcare. The group also expanded into parking solutions and facility management services, diversifying its portfolio beyond traditional sectors.

2012

Industrial Manufacturing

Establishment of Saudi Builmix LLC for ready-mix concrete production and Samara Chemical Additives for industrial chemical solutions. The group achieved significant growth in manufacturing capabilities, serving major construction projects across the Kingdom.

2024

The Present Day

Seven specialized companies operating across Saudi Arabia and the GCC, with a diversified portfolio spanning refrigeration, real estate, healthcare, parking solutions, ready-mix concrete, and chemical additives. Samara Group continues to build the future of Saudi Arabia while honoring the family values and business excellence that began in 1992.
