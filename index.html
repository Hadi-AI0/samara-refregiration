About Us

Who We Are

Four Decades of Business Excellence

40+ Years of Excellence

Samara Group is a prominent family-based business group based in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, with a strategic presence across the GCC region.

Since our foundation in 1984, we have grown into a diversified conglomerate operating in key sectors including commercial refrigeration, real estate development, healthcare management, parking solutions, ready-mix concrete, and chemical additives.

Our commitment to quality, innovation, and sustainable growth drives our operations, ensuring we deliver value to our partners, clients, and the communities we serve.

Our Companies

Samara H-VACR

HVAC & Refrigeration

Leading provider of heating, ventilation, air conditioning, and refrigeration solutions delivering innovative climate control systems for residential, commercial, and industrial applications across the Kingdom.

When we laid the first stone of Samara Group in 1984, our vision was simple yet ambitious: to build a legacy rooted in integrity, quality, and service to our nation. Four decades later, that vision continues to guide every decision we make.

We are not just a business group; we are a family committed to contributing to the Kingdom's growth and to the prosperity of the communities we serve. Our journey from a single refrigeration company to a diversified conglomerate is a testament to the trust of our partners and the dedication of our team.

As Saudi Arabia strides boldly toward Vision 2030, we stand ready to play our part, building the infrastructure, healthcare, and innovations that will shape the future of generations to come.

Our Journey

Milestones of Growth Since 1984

Key moments that shaped our legacy

1984

The Foundation

Samara Group was founded in Riyadh, beginning its journey with a focus on commercial refrigeration services for local businesses.

1990

Regional Expansion

Expanded operations across the Kingdom, establishing a reputation for reliability and securing major contracts with leading retailers and food processors.

2001

Diversification Era

Launched Samara Real Estate & Contracting and entered the healthcare sector with Saryryah Healthcare, marking a new chapter of growth.

2012

Industrial Growth

Established Saudi Builmix LLC, expanding into heavy industry and manufacturing to support the Kingdom's infrastructure boom.

2020

Digital Transformation

Embraced smart technologies and launched Samara Parking Solutions, delivering intelligent urban mobility systems aligned with Saudi Vision 2030.

2026

Future Vision

Continuing to innovate with sustainable construction materials and advanced parking solutions, strengthening our role in shaping the Kingdom's future.
