About Saudi Builmix

Saudi Builmix is a premier provider of construction chemicals and advanced building solutions. We are dedicated to enhancing the quality, durability, and sustainability of construction projects across the Kingdom. Our range of products, including Microsilica, Fly Ash, and GGBFS, meets the highest international standards.

We deliver high-performance, cost-effective cement replacement materials for durable concrete. Count on us for expert technical support on mix designs and reliable supply throughout Saudi Arabia. Our materials are trusted by professionals who demand consistency, performance, and reliability.

Our Products

High-performance construction materials

Microsilica

Builmix Microsilica is a high-pozzolanic activity densified microsilica for concrete and mortar, providing exceptional strength and durability.

Fly Ash

Builmix Fly Ash is a residue of coal combustion that occurs at power generation plants, used as a supplementary cementitious material.

GGBFS

Builmix GGBFS is a powder Ground Granulated Blast Furnace Slag that is used in concrete to enhance strength and reduce environmental impact.

Industries We Serve

Serving the Concrete & Construction Industry. Builmix supplies materials to: Ready-Mix Concrete Plants, Precast Concrete Manufacturers, Infrastructure & Mega Projects, Cement Product Factories, and Industrial & Commercial Developments.

Our materials are trusted by professionals who demand consistency, performance, and reliability in every project.

Quality & Sustainability

Performance with Responsibility

At Builmix, quality control is a core principle. Our materials undergo strict testing to meet relevant standards while contributing to reduced cement usage and lower CO₂ emissions.

By using cement replacement materials, our clients achieve improved durability and service life, lower environmental impact, and compliance with modern sustainability goals.
