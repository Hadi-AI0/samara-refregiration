About Saudi Builmix

Saudi Builmix is a premier provider of construction chemicals and advanced building solutions.

We are dedicated to enhancing the quality, durability, and sustainability of construction projects across the Kingdom. Our range of products, including Microsilica, Fly Ash, and GGBFS, meets the highest international standards.

We deliver high-performance, cost-effective cement replacement materials for durable concrete. Count on us for expert technical support on mix designs and reliable supply throughout Saudi Arabia.

Our Products

We deliver high-performance, cost-effective cement replacement materials

Microsilica

Builmix Microsilica is a high-pozzolanic activity densified microsilica for concrete and mortar, ensuring superior strength and durability.

Fly Ash

Builmix Fly Ash is a residue of coal combustion that occurs at power generation plants, used to enhance concrete workability and reduce heat generation.

GGBFS

Builmix GGBFS is a powder Ground Granulated Blast Furnace Slag that is used in concrete to improve long-term strength and resistance to chemical attack.
