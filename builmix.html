About Saudi Builmix

Saudi Builmix is a premier producer of high-quality ready-mix concrete and specialized chemical additives for the construction industry. With state-of-the-art batching plants across Saudi Arabia, we deliver superior concrete solutions for major infrastructure, commercial, and residential projects.

Our commitment to quality, innovation, and sustainability has made us a trusted partner for contractors, developers, and government entities throughout the Kingdom. We combine advanced technology with expert engineering to meet the most demanding construction requirements.
