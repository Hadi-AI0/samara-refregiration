About Samara Parking Solutions

We didn't rename ourselves.. we redefined our ambition. Since day one of our journey of innovation and manufacturing, we worked on providing advanced and suitable solutions for the various needs of developing communities and decreasing percentages of tensions resulting from congestion and the multiplying numbers of vehicles.

This is our main drive in partnership with Qingdao GG Lifters Co. to resolve said problems through our Research and Development systems administered by experienced and scientifically-competent engineers in the automatic parking engineering field.

We have already achieved international accreditation of our automatic units via CE certified according to EC machinery directive 2006/42/CE to guarantee to present to you the best automatic units aligning with the most accurate details accredited internationally.
