About Samara Parking Solutions

Since day one of our journey of innovation and manufacturing, we worked on providing advanced and suitable solutions for the various needs of developing communities and decreasing percentages of tensions resulting from congestion and the multiplying numbers of vehicles. This is our main drive in partnership with Qingdao GG Lifters Co. to resolve said problems through our Research and Development systems administered by experienced and scientifically-competent engineers in the automatic parking engineering field.

Simultaneously, we have already achieved international accreditation of our automatic units via CE certified according to EC machinery directive 2006/42/CE to guarantee to present to you the best automatic units aligning with the most accurate details accredited internationally.

Our Solutions

Advanced parking systems for modern cities

Dependent Parking Solutions

Space-efficient systems where vehicles are parked in multiple levels using mechanical assistance, maximizing capacity in limited areas.

Independent Parking Solutions

Advanced systems allowing each vehicle to be accessed independently without moving other cars, perfect for high-traffic facilities.

Car Elevators

Vertical transportation systems for vehicles, enabling multi-level parking structures with minimal footprint and maximum efficiency.

Automatic Solutions

Fully automated parking systems with robotic technology, providing seamless parking experience with enhanced security and space optimization.

Installation & Maintenance

Professional installation services and comprehensive maintenance programs ensuring optimal performance and longevity of your parking systems.

After-Sale Services

Dedicated support team providing responsive after-sale services, spare parts, and technical assistance for all our parking solutions.

Research & Development

Our R&D systems are administered by experienced and scientifically-competent engineers in the automatic parking engineering field. We continuously innovate to provide advanced and suitable solutions for the various needs of developing communities.

We have achieved international accreditation of our automatic units via CE certification according to EC machinery directive 2006/42/CE, guaranteeing the best automatic units aligning with the most accurate international standards.
