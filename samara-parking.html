About Samara Parking

Samara Parking is a leading parking management company in Saudi Arabia, delivering comprehensive parking solutions that combine operational excellence with cutting-edge technology. We manage parking facilities for shopping malls, office buildings, hospitals, airports, and residential complexes.

Our team of experienced professionals ensures smooth daily operations, maximum revenue optimization, and exceptional customer service. We leverage smart parking technologies including automated payment systems, license plate recognition, and real-space monitoring to enhance the parking experience.

With a commitment to safety, efficiency, and innovation, Samara Parking continues to set industry standards in parking facility management throughout the Kingdom.
