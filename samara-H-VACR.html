About Samara H-VACR

Since our establishment in 1984, Samara H-VACR has been at the forefront of commercial and industrial refrigeration and HVAC solutions in Saudi Arabia. As the founding company of Samara Group, we have built a reputation for excellence, reliability, and innovation.

Our expertise spans across design, installation, and maintenance of comprehensive refrigeration and HVAC systems for supermarkets, cold storage warehouses, food processing plants, commercial buildings, and industrial facilities throughout the GCC region.

We combine cutting-edge technology with decades of experience to deliver energy-efficient, sustainable cooling and climate control solutions that meet the unique challenges of the Middle Eastern climate.
