About Samara H-VACR

Samara H-VACR (Heating, Ventilation, Air Conditioning, and Refrigeration) is a leading manufacturer and provider of comprehensive climate control solutions within the Samara Group. Established as part of Samara Group's diversified portfolio, we specialize in designing, manufacturing, and installing advanced HVAC&R systems for residential, commercial, and industrial applications across the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility combines cutting-edge technology with rigorous quality standards to produce energy-efficient cooling and refrigeration equipment. From central air conditioning systems to specialized industrial refrigeration units, we deliver reliable solutions that meet the unique demands of the region's challenging climate while adhering to international safety and environmental standards.
