About Saryryah Healthcare

Established in 901, Saryryah Healthcare has grown into a leading property development and construction company in Saudi Arabia. We specialize in creating exceptional residential, commercial, and mixed-use developments that enhance communities and deliver lasting value.

Our contracting division undertakes major infrastructure and building projects, combining technical expertise with project management excellence to deliver on time and within budget.

We are committed to sustainable development practices and innovative construction methodologies that meet international standards while respecting local architectural heritage.
