About Salmiya Oasis

Redefining Hospitality Excellence

Salmiya Oasis is a premier hospitality management company dedicated to creating unforgettable experiences for guests seeking luxury, comfort, and authentic Arabian hospitality. As part of the Samara Group portfolio, we combine traditional values with modern service standards.

Our portfolio includes exquisite resorts, boutique hotels, and recreational facilities designed to provide guests with serene escapes and memorable stays. We pride ourselves on attention to detail, personalized service, and creating environments where guests can relax, rejuvenate, and connect.
