About Salmiya Oasis

Salmiya Oasis is Al-Khobar's premier event destination, offering an expansive 10,000 square meters of versatile space designed for corporate events, conferences, exhibitions, and large-scale gatherings. Located in the heart of Al-Khobar, our state-of-the-art facility combines elegant design with modern amenities to create unforgettable experiences.

Our venue features multiple configurable halls equipped with advanced audiovisual systems, professional lighting, and high-speed connectivity. Whether hosting a product launch, annual conference, trade exhibition, or corporate celebration, our experienced events team provides comprehensive support from planning to execution, ensuring every detail exceeds expectations.
